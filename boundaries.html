Boundaries

Never reply to work emails after work hours. Coworkers and parents usually forget you have a life outside your classroom.Responding to emails after work hours is not part of your contract.
Do not be afraid to direct parents to admins when you are approached about a situation out of your control. Your priority are your students, not the entire school.
during lunch and breaks DO NOT grade or lesson plan during your breaks. Use this time to recharge.
Avoid overcommitment. Yes you will be enthusiastic and happy to help in any way especially in your first year, but this will cause burn out.
Saying no is OKAY. Again, your priorities are your students. If you are asked to take on additional work and you canâ€™t, say no. Without hesitation.
Remember, having clear boundaries will help you gain more respect from your environment than being the person who says yes to everything, while also saying your mental health.
